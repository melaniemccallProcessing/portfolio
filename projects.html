The Middle Man

After acquiring a new supplier, my client needed a way to automate placing orders to the Dropshipper from Shopify. This Node.js app has three jobs:
1: Use Shopify's webhooks to receive data on placed orders and transform it to be sent to the Dropshipper's API
2: After placing order, check for updates on shipping and notify the customer on Shopify
3: Daily updates on products availability based on Dropshipper's product datafeed

Languages Used & Tools:
Node.js, XML
